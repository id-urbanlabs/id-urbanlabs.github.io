UrbanLabs focuses on urban systems and spatial analytics, with emphasis on data-driven planning, sustainability, and equitable development.

Our team collaborates with national ministries, research institutes, and international organizations, working at the intersection of policy, technology, and urban transformation.

The official website for Urban Labs Indonesia is now live — a platform under the Urban Labs initiative to share work, ideas, and updates on data-driven urban policy and innovation.

29-Apr-2025: Urban Labs Internship Project Concept Approved   (more)

The Deputy Director for Urban Affairs approved the concept to launch an undergraduate research and internship project under Urban Labs, aimed at supporting urban policy and innovation through close collaboration with universities. The Bureau of Human Resources has expressed readiness to coordinate the deployment of internship requests and placements.
